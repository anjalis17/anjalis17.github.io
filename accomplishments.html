Accomplishments

Highlights of our Journey in Robotics

APR 2019

Won VEX IQ Teamwork Challenge Division Second Place

This was our last year participating as a VEX IQ team. After qualifying for worlds for the first time, we were ecstatic and went into the compeition with no expectations other than to try our best. Between State and Worlds, we invested hours every day to rebuild our robot, improve our autonomous, and drive, in preparation for the higher-level competition. Our hard work paid off, and we made it to division finals and managed to place second in our division!

JUL 2019

Hosted VEX IQ In-Person Summer Camp

As our time in VEX IQ came to an end, we wanted to provide the next generation of VEX IQ competitiors a headstart to the competition season by teaching them the skills we learned during our competitive years.\ From our own experience in VEX IQ, we know that starting robotics is hard without having the proper knowledge.
