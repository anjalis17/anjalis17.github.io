Who We Are

We are VRC Team 322A Synergy, a team of five passionate high school juniors based in Sammamish, WA. We believe that hardwork, perseverance, and having a "can-do" attitude are the keys to success, and we hope to inspire younger students in our community by sharing what we have learned throughout our journey. Our mission is to empower elementary and middle school students to discover their interests and find their talents in the STEM fields, by providing them with the tools and guidance they need to succeed in competitive VEX IQ robotics.

Anjali Sreenivas

Anjali is a junior at Tesla STEM High School who is captivated by the STEM fields, from computer programming and artificial intelligence to biology and medicine. Her journey in competitive robotics began in 8th grade, where she fell in love with the intense competition environment and the problem-solving adventures that arise every step of the way. In addition to robotics, Anjali also enjoys singing, competing in Science Olympiad, and cooking new foods. She's looking forward to another exciting robotics season with her teammates and can't wait to help younger students succeed in VEX IQ!

Atharva began competitive robotics in 7th grade through VEX IQ, and he is currently participating in his third year of VEX VRC. He looks forward to another successful and enjoyable season this year.
