Who We Are

We are VRC Team 322A Synergy, a team of five passionate high school juniors based in Sammamish, WA. We're on a mission to spread our passion and empower younger students to discover their interests and find their talents in the STEM fields, through competitive VEX IQ robotics.

Anjali Sreenivas

Anjali is a junior at Tesla STEM High School.

Atharva Limaye

Atharva is a rising junior at Eastlake High school. He is passionate about engineering, computer science, and robotics. Atharva began competitive robotics in 7th grade in Vex IQ, and is currently participating in his third year of VRC. He looks forward to another successful and enjoyable season this year.

Dhruv Bansal

Dhruv is in 11th grade at Eastlake High School. He is extremely passionate about robotics and anything to do with technology. His robotics career started in 7th grade in Vex IQ and has continued till starting VRC in freshman year. Outside of robotics, he enjoys photography and driving.
