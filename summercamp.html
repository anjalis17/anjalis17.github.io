Our Summer Camps

A fun introduction to robotics!

Overview

If you are planning to join the Vex community, sign up for our summer camp! Our course will aim to teach students about the Vex IQ pieces, common structures such as a four-bar, SnapCAD, and basic programming.

Course Overview

Our two week summer camp covers almost all of the basic knowledge required to be successful in VEX IQ. Through lectures and interactive learning, students will be able to learn how to design, build, and code.

Design

Students will learn how to use SnapCAD, a online VEX CAD platform that allows students to design a robot digitally. Learning to design a robot digitally is an important skill to learn. Designing a robot digitally also gives teams more creative freedom to play around with different designs before choosing one to build.

Build

Students will learn how the basic pieces in VEX IQ and their uses. After learning about the different VEX pieces and their uses, students can then use the CAD that they designed to build a simple robot. Students will usually spend a few days learning the basics to VEX IQ pieces and structural design. Students will also learn about different building concepts to further their knowledge in robotics before the competition season.

Code

Students will learn basic C++ coding and algorithsms using Vexcode VEX IQ. Coding is one crucial aspect to VEX IQ, and this summer camp dedicates two days to ensure that students can grasp basic coding concepts that will later give them a huge advantage in the competition season. In past camps, students have learned basic coding concepts such as conditionals, loops, and functions.
